### Implementation Approach
**layout:** eo_single_column

**Proven Migration Methodology for Minimal Business Disruption**

- **Phase 1: Planning & Pilot (Weeks 1-3)**
  - Conduct migration planning workshops and assess current environment
  - Configure Google Admin console, SSO, and security policies
  - Pilot with 25 IT and early adopter users to validate workflows
- **Phase 2: Department Phased Migration (Weeks 4-10)**
  - Migrate 500 mailboxes from Exchange to Gmail in 5 waves of 100 users
  - Transfer 3 TB of file server data to Google Drive with permissions preservation
  - Migrate 150 distribution lists to Google Groups with automated provisioning
- **Phase 3: Training & Adoption (Weeks 8-12)**
  - Deliver administrator training on Google Admin console and GAM scripting
  - Conduct 10 virtual end-user training sessions covering Gmail, Drive, Docs, Meet
  - Provide 2 weeks of go-live hypercare support with dedicated help desk

**SPEAKER NOTES:**

*Risk Mitigation:*
- Pilot phase validates migration tools and user workflows before full rollout
- Phased approach limits impact to 100 users at a time reducing risk
- Coexistence period allows fallback to Exchange if critical issues arise

*Success Factors:*
- Executive sponsorship and communication plan driving user adoption
- Dedicated migration team available for cutover weekends
- Clear training and support resources for self-service help

*Talking Points:*
- Pilot proves Gmail and Drive work with existing workflows
- Phased migration allows course correction based on feedback
- Training focuses on quick wins like real-time collaboration features
